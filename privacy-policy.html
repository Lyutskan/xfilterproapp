Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

X Feed Filter Pro ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password. This is used solely to authenticate you and sync your data across devices.

Tweet fingerprints: We store anonymized identifiers (tweet IDs or content hashes) of posts you have seen on X. These are used to filter previously viewed content. No tweet text, images, or media are stored.

Usage statistics: We collect aggregate counts (number of posts hidden per day) to display your personal statistics. This data is tied to your account and not shared with third parties.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your full browsing history
  • We do not read, store, or transmit the content of tweets
  • We do not collect financial or payment information (payments are processed by Stripe)
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or third parties
  • We do not track you across websites other than x.com and twitter.com

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the core functionality of the Extension: filtering previously seen posts on X, syncing your filter history across your devices, and displaying your personal usage statistics.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (supabase.com), a cloud database provider based in the EU. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Payments

Pro subscriptions are processed by Stripe (stripe.com). We do not store credit card numbers or payment details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via the Extension or email.
